PS01. Traffic Management in WCE using Video Surveillance
Allow parking for two and four-wheelers in the given parking area. If any vehicle is out of that area, generate an SMS to the owner. Report the location to the authority through video surveillance and process it using ML. You may use RFID cards or a number capturing system to maintain a database of all vehicles, owner information, and mobile numbers. (Participants can extend the problem statement and use sensors to buzz the alarm for parking in a non-authorized area)
PS02. Green Calculator
Development of Green index in the laboratory Input: Data can be assumed for preparing a prototype; 1) A computer laboratory with equipment details in terms of : - electric power requirement and heat emissions through the equipment considering the age of the equipment, total numbers and arrangement of the types of equipments - Average expected human crowd capacity/compactness of the laboratory against a window and air ventilation - And similar other parameters, that are directly or indirectly applicable in the regard 2) Sample rubric template to define Green index indicating harm to the humans because of compact laboratories Expected Output: % Green Index of the laboratory with its interpretation Application: Assessment metric for environmental audits for computer labs Significance of undertaking the project: As per standard norms, the utility rooms/ laboratories need to follow specific environmental rules. The judgemental violation of the rules is required to be quantified. Development of the Green Calculator may help to take corrective measures that support healthier lives of the users working in the laboratory.
PS03.Development of “WCE-Verification” portal
Input: Request by an ex-WCE student for a document (student who has already passed out from campus may request for a copy of their mark list, TC, Recommendation etc.) Expected Output: Verified document from the WCE portal :- - The verification process must involve the student’s unique ID linked with their Adhar number. The reason to involve Adhar number is to further help the exploration of the student with the global Indian database. - A blockchain may help to maintain the data flow of an individual student.The overall academic record might be required for future transactions/interactions.
PS04. Audio Forgery
The increase in low cost digital audio recorders, mobile applications and accelerating growth of the audio-based Internet of Things (IoT) has initiated ease in obtaining speech and audio data .This data can be used to identify human traits, implement voice authentication systems and to develop voice-based embedded systems.
PS05. Secure Online Exam using Blockchain technology in WCE
The aim is to secure question papers uploaded by teachers on the portal without getting tampered. And in the same way, whatever answers have been uploaded by students should not get altered. Only teachers and students should be allowed to view the data, eliminating the third party like exam cell. The solution can apply to T1 and TE.
PS06. E-waste management and disposal system using machine learning
Growing infrastructure and setup based on electronic devices and vehicles is a very effective solution for natural resources like petrol ,diesel and CNG based systems.However, it also has some crucial problems like how to manage/dump electronic devices/systems used in electric vehicles (like batteries used for charging purposes). Try to find out an effective solution for this problem statement using a machine learning /IoT based system (or any other suitable technology)
PS07. Network setup and management for type C/D corporation for a smart city initiative
The problem statement is typically for only Type C /D corporations like Sangli, Satara, Kolhapur etc. As they have a limited coverage area and have limited resources and infrastructure , device a solution trying to find out an effective solution over basic problems( traffic management, parking system, water resources management, solid waste management, drainage system etc.)
